About Us: Our Story

When the perfect location opened up in historic downtown Seguin, Mary Jo and Lyndon Langford opted out of early retirement and opened Court Street Coffee Shop, a community-gathering venue offering great coffee and espresso drinks, gourmet beverages and ice cream, healthy food options not readily found in Seguin, a grab-n-go lunch menu, and a stage and gallery space to showcase local talent. The atmosphere encourages old friends to gather and new friends to meet.
